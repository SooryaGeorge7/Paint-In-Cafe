How Does It Work?

Be Inspired!

Are you looking for a creative activity to do in the city?
We invite you to bring yourself or company to have the unique experience of watercolors painting while eating at our Cafe!

Get Creative!

All equipment needed for the experience will be provided to you when you have ordered food from the cafe.
Equipments provided:
watercolor paint, painting paper pad, brushes, glass with water to clean brush, mixing palette and a sponge

Eat And Paint!

Paint-In café is equipped to provide you with light meals, baked goods and drinks as we definitely do not want you to be hungry or thirsty while letting your creative juices flow!
Please note: Additional cost of 5 euros pp will be added to your bill from the cafe for the painting experience.

Beginner Friendly!

No Experience?
No problem!
This environment is curated for having fun! If anyone is in need of help, the staff are there to help and they also provide tracing templates if needed.

No age restrictions!

All ages are welcome!
There is no age limit to creativity and imagination!
Please note:
Kids below 12 years old must be accompanied by atleast 1 adult.

Bookings!

Bookings are only needed if you are a group of more than 10+ people otherwise it works as a first come, first serve basis. Please go to Event Enquiry page and fill in our form.
NB! Please submit an enquiry in advance(atleast 2 weeks ahead) as there is a limit to 2 events per weekend.
