How does it work?

Once you visit our cafe and order from our menu, Your meal with come with a wide range of colorful watercolor paint, a mixing palette, drawing pad, a watercolor brush pen and a water container. Take this opportunity to paint your heart out for 2 hours while enjoying a cup of your favorite beverage and a delicious meal from our café menu! Our friendly staff are always available for any assistance. Please note: A fee of 5€ pp will be added to your final bill for the given experience. You don't need to book to enter the café, our café works on a first come, first serve basis.
