Paint-In Café

The Spot For Anyone In Need For A Creative Outlet!

Cafe Update:
Now open Friday to Sunday, 8AM to 8PM.

How Does It Work?

Be Inspired!

Bring yourself or company to have the unique experience of painting while eating at our Cafe!

Get Creative!

All equipment needed for the experience will be provided to you when you have ordered food from the cafe.
Please note: Additional cost of 5 euros pp will be added to your bill from the cafe for the painting experience.

Eat And Paint!

Our café is equipped to provide you with light meals, baked goods and drinks as we definitely do not want you to be hungry during the experience.

Beginner Friendly!

No Experience?No problem, this environment is curated for having fun! If anyone is in need of help, the staff are there to help and they also provide tracing templates if needed.

No age restrictions!

All ages welcome!
Please note:Kids below 12 years old must be accompanied by atleast 1 adult.

Bookings!

Bookings are only needed if you are a group of more than 10+ people otherwise it works as a first come, first serve basis. Please go to Event Enquiry page and fill in our form.
NB! Please submit an enquiry in advance(atleast 2 weeks ahead) as there is a limit to 2 events per weekend.
