About Paint-In Cafe

Paint-in Cafe is a business run by Sunny Marie Carsens. Sunny has been an artist for the last 15 years. Her Lifelong dream of opening up this cafe came true in July,2006. It was important for her to incorporate art in her cafe. Since watercolours is her favorite medium, this unique experience offered to our customers has set us apart in Dublin, Ireland.
This "one of a kind" experience is ideal for a group of friends, family or couple. We also host small events such as school outings, bridal shower or team building exercises.
