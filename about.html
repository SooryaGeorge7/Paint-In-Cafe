Our Story

Cafe Update:
Now open Friday to Sunday, 8AM to 8PM.

Who is the owner?
Paint-in Cafe is a business run by owner Sunny Marie Carsens. Sunny has been an artist for the last 15 years. Her Lifelong dream of opening up this cafe came true in July,2006.

What sets us apart?
It was important for Sunny to incorporate art in her cafe because art gave her healing like nothing else in the world. Since watercolours is her favorite medium, this unique experience offered to our customers has set us apart in Dublin, Ireland.She hopes the experience is therapeutic to anyone who visits! Our café also serves up fresh food every day and it is therefore an added experience to be able to eat while letting your creative juices flow!

Who can come?
This "one of a kind" experience is ideal for a group of friends, family or couples. We also host small events such as school outings, bridal shower or team building exercises.Go to Events Enquiry Page for groups of 10+ people or if you want to host an event.

We look forward to you immersing yourself in this experience so please be sure to visit us!
